About Me

I am an engineering student and developer focused on offline-first applications and local AI architectures. I enjoy exploring systems-level Windows integration, writing low-overhead desktop agents, and building web systems that run high-performance algorithms locally or on the edge.

My philosophy is centered around creating applications that don't depend on expensive cloud APIs, ensuring user privacy, speed, and reliability. Whether it's capturing and routing WASAPI audio channels, optimizing GGUF models for low-end CPUs, or designing timetabling constraint-satisfaction solvers in React, I focus on performance and visual appeal.
